Why Recruit At DA-IICT

DA-IICT is the first university in India to offer undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in the area of Information and Communication Technology. The degrees offered are various : B.Tech.(ICT), M.Tech.(ICT), M.Sc.(IT),M.Sc.(ICT in ARD) and M.Des.(Communication Design). All the programs have their specific distinguished values and qualities and students undergo a rigorous learning process based on the present industrial demands and latest research areas, enabling them to immediately take up diverse roles in industrial organizations with élan. The unique syllabus grants versatility to the students, ensuring that they have professional knowledge of all fields of ICT. The industry is always in need of highly skilled fresh talent, and this need is addressed by DA-IICT's comprehensive and long term approach to education with a highly charged and professional atmosphere.

At DA-IICT, we have faculty unparalleled at both the national and international levels, thus creating a hub of intelligent, hardworking and technically proficient people with creative and uncommon ideas. The learning environment gives the students sufficient exposure to gain a significant edge over others, ensuring that they are industry ready when they join and able to integrate with the corporate world with confidence. For all these reasons, DA-IICT has long been one of the most preferred institutes by recruiters - successfully delivering candidates to various top corporations - and has been accepted as an institution of significant repute. And so, every year fresh recruits are placed in top-notch companies; recruits who continue to deliver high level of performance to their employers.
